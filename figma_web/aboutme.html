About Me



Hello, I am Rishita! I am currently a rising junior at Parsons School of Design, NY for BFA Product Design. I plan to major in Industrial/ Product Design & minor in Communication Design by 2024.

I enjoy the process of making a product, and try to weave a playful and interactive aesthetic to my designs. I believe in creating an impact with my designs. I am always ready to learn new things and challenge myself. You will see from my work that my creative energy and the ability to solve problems are great tools in any setting I encounter.

Contact me for freelance, full-time or collaborative opportunities!
